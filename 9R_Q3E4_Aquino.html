5 Most Popular Characters from Jujutsu Kaisen





1. Itadori Yuji





- Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen, who is also the vessel or body that Sukuna resides in. Despite being the most important character since the beginning of the series, he is ordered by Gojo to be executed after giving Sukuna full power, which is done by finding all 20 of his fingers.





2. Kugisaki Nobara





- Kugisaki is introduced as a 1st year of Jujutsu high, being accompanied by Fushiguro Megumi. Although not being fond of many people, she respects Yuji and Fushiguro very dearly.





3. Fushiguro Megumi







- As a 2nd year, Fushiguro is given responsibility by Satoru Gojo to look out for Kugisaki and Itadori. He owns the most potential out of the 3 and mastered his technique of summoning two shikigami at the same time.





4. Satoru Gojo







- Gojo is known for mentoring all three students, Itadori, Kugisaki, and Fushiguro. Revealing his eyes would result in draining his energy, thus using a blindfold, only removing it in critical situations.





5. Sukuna







- Sukuna is the main antagonist of the series, being able to possess anyone at will every vessel he enters. Though being the most powerful with all 20 fingers, he is easily controlled by Yuji who can switch to him without difficulty.
